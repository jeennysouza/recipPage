  • total aproximadamente 10 min

igredientes

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to tast
  • pepper ro taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • optional fillings cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meants, herbs

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixced. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surfoce Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin ta set at the edges but are stillsightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half af the omelette
  4. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it anto a plate
  5. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
